Top 5 Dog Breeds Perfect for Children

These dogs are also perfect for small families who needs someone to cheer everyone up!, These breeds are smart and they'll never fail to make you smile and say "awe"

Image Breed Description
Labrador Retriever The Labrador Retriever or simply Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog. It was developed in the United Kingdom from fishing dogs imported from the colony of Newfoundland, and was named after the Labrador region of that colony.
Poodle The Poodle, called the Pudel in German and the Caniche in French, is a breed of water dog. The breed is divided into four varieties based on size, the Standard Poodle, Medium Poodle, Miniature Poodle and Toy Poodle, although the Medium Poodle variety is not universally recognised.
Bulldog The Bulldog is a British breed of dog of mastiff type. It may also be known as the English Bulldog or British Bulldog. It is of medium size, a muscular, hefty dog with a wrinkled face and a distinctive pushed-in nose.
Vizsla The Vizsla has a lively disposition but a gentle manner, and is both loyal and affectionate. The breed is also obedient, confident, and smart. Vizslas form close bonds with family members and are able to learn new tricks quickly. They have a short coat, so their grooming routine is quite low maintenance.
Newfoundland The Newfoundland is a large working dog. They can be black, brown, grey, or white. However, in the Dominion of Newfoundland, before it became part of the confederation of Canada, only black and Landseer coloured dogs were considered to be proper members of the breed
